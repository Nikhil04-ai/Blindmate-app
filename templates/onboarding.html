Welcome to BlindMate

Welcome! BlindMate is your AI-powered navigation assistant designed specifically for visually impaired users. This tutorial will teach you how to use all the features safely and effectively.

What BlindMate Can Do

  • Smart object detection with non-overlapping speech announcements
  • Universal navigation to ANY location worldwide via Google Maps
  • Voice wake word support - just say "Hey BlindMate"
  • 15 language support including global languages
  • 5 customizable voice tones (friendly, formal, energetic, calm, robotic)
  • Mobile double-tap gesture for touch devices
  • Battery-optimized GPS tracking with smart frequency adjustment

Safety First

BlindMate is an assistive tool to enhance your navigation. Always use your white cane, guide dog, or other mobility aids alongside BlindMate. Never rely solely on technology for navigation safety.

Voice Commands

BlindMate responds to natural voice commands. You can speak normally - no need for specific phrases.

Try These Commands

Universal Navigation (Works Anywhere):
• "Take me to Eiffel Tower"
• "Navigate to Central Park New York"
• "Go to Times Square"
• "Directions to hospital nearby"
Wake Word Commands:
• "Hey BlindMate, start object detection"
• "Hey BlindMate, what's around me?"
• "Hey BlindMate, change language to Hindi"
• "Hey BlindMate, speak in calm tone"
3

Object Detection

BlindMate uses AI to identify objects, people, and obstacles in real-time through your device's camera.

Enhanced Detection Features

  • Smart Announcements: 1.5-second delays prevent overlapping voices
  • 80+ Objects: People, vehicles, furniture, animals, and more
  • Distance Awareness: "Person ahead at 2 meters"
  • Real-time Processing: Continuous monitoring during navigation
  • Anti-Spam Protection: Intelligent announcement throttling

Detection Tips

  • Hold your device steady for better accuracy
  • Detection works best in good lighting
  • Use "Hey BlindMate" wake word for hands-free commands
  • On mobile devices, double-tap screen to activate voice commands
  • BlindMate prevents speech overlap with smart timing
4

Navigation Features

Get step-by-step walking directions to any destination with voice guidance throughout your journey.

Universal Navigation Flow

Step 1: Say ANY destination worldwide
"Hey BlindMate, take me to Eiffel Tower"

Step 2: BlindMate confirms location
"Should I start navigation to Eiffel Tower, Paris?"

Step 3: GPS tracking with battery optimization
"Turn left in 50 meters onto Champs-Élysées"

Step 4: Automatic arrival detection
"You have arrived at Eiffel Tower"

Navigation Commands:
• "Stop navigation" - Emergency stop button
• "Repeat last instruction" - Hear directions again
• "How far to destination?" - Distance check
• Smart rerouting when you deviate >50 meters from route
