Welcome to BlindMate

Welcome! BlindMate is your AI-powered navigation assistant designed specifically for visually impaired users. This tutorial will teach you how to use all the features safely and effectively.

What BlindMate Can Do

  • Real-time object detection and obstacle warnings
  • Voice-controlled navigation to any location
  • Step-by-step walking directions
  • Multilingual support in 7 Indian languages
  • Emergency assistance features

Safety First

BlindMate is an assistive tool to enhance your navigation. Always use your white cane, guide dog, or other mobility aids alongside BlindMate. Never rely solely on technology for navigation safety.

2

Voice Commands

BlindMate responds to natural voice commands. You can speak normally - no need for specific phrases.

Try These Commands

Navigation Commands:
• "Take me to the library"
• "Navigate to the nearest restaurant"
• "Where is the bathroom?"
• "Guide me home"
Detection Commands:
• "What's in front of me?"
• "Describe my surroundings"
• "Any obstacles ahead?"
• "Start detection mode"
3

Object Detection

BlindMate uses AI to identify objects, people, and obstacles in real-time through your device's camera.

What It Detects

  • People: "Person ahead at 2 meters"
  • Vehicles: "Car approaching from the right"
  • Objects: "Chair in front, table to your left"
  • Animals: "Dog walking nearby"
  • Barriers: "Fence blocking the path"

Detection Tips

  • Hold your device steady for better accuracy
  • Detection works best in good lighting
  • BlindMate announces important objects automatically
  • You can ask for detailed descriptions anytime
4

Navigation Features

Get step-by-step walking directions to any destination with voice guidance throughout your journey.

Navigation Flow

Step 1: Say your destination
"Take me to the library"

Step 2: BlindMate confirms
"Navigating to Central Library, 0.5 km away"

Step 3: Follow voice instructions
"In 50 meters, turn right onto Main Street"

Step 4: Arrival confirmation
"You have arrived at your destination"

Navigation Commands:
• "Stop navigation"
• "Repeat last instruction"
• "How far to destination?"
• "Find alternate route"
5

Emergency Features

BlindMate includes safety features to help you in emergency situations.

Emergency Commands

Immediate Help:
• "Emergency" - Activates emergency mode
• "Call for help" - Contacts emergency services
• "Share my location" - Sends location to contacts
• "I'm lost" - Provides detailed location info

Emergency Mode Features

  • Loud audio alerts to attract attention
  • Automatic location sharing
  • Emergency contact notification
  • Continuous obstacle warnings
  • Easy access to emergency services
